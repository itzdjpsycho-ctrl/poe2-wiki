# Path of Exile 2 Current Meta — Patch 0.5 (Return of the Ancients)

**League:** Runes of Aldur
**Patch:** 0.5.4 (as of late June 2026; 0.5.3 dropped June 18, 2026)
**Theme:** Runeforging, Expedition overhaul, first-ever Challenge League with Challenges

## Top Tier Builds (S-Tier)

### Lightning Arrow Deadeye (Ranger)
- **#1 most played build in 0.5** — 34%+ of top players on Deadeye; 80% of those use Lightning Arrow
- Absolute king of map clear speed
- Lightning Arrow bounces and chains — hits enormous density
- Great for budget and expensive versions
- If speed is your goal, this is the build

### Twister (varies — often Ranger/Monk)
- Completely untouched by 0.5 nerfs
- Strong league starter — exceptional projectile speed scaling, wide AOE
- Good at both mapping and bossing
- Considered the "most overpowered untouched" build this patch

### Ice Shot Deadeye (Ranger)
- S-tier; strong single target with AoE ice spread
- Deadeye ascendancy buffs projectiles greatly

### Ice Strike Martial Artist (Monk)
- Top tier; Monk's strong melee/elemental hybrid
- Invoker or Martial Artist ascendancy

### Explosive Shot Invoker (Monk)
- High damage explosive projectile
- Invoker is one of the strongest Monk ascendancies

### Whirling Assault Titan (Warrior)
- Warbringer/Titan spec
- Strong attack-based build; survived 0.5 untouched or buffed

### Grenade Totems Warbringer (Warrior)
- **Gold standard league starter**
- Low button presses, massive armor breaks
- Excellent for beginners — tanky, consistent damage

### Demon Recoup Infernalist (Witch)
- S-tier; life/minion hybrid with recoup mechanics

## Notable Nerfs in 0.5
- **Poison Pathfinder** — hit hard; multiple poison scaling sources nerfed
- **Energy Shield recovery** — nerfed across the board (ES recharge rate/delay)
- **Blood Mage leech builds** — nerfed
- **Infernal Legion minion builds** — reduced power
- **Thorns builds** — nerfed

## Notable Buffs in 0.5
- **Runes** — meaningful buffs to Runeforging output
- **Attack-based builds** generally buffed or untouched
- **Deadeye** — still dominant, slightly improved projectile mechanics
- **Titan/Warbringer** — attack builds doing well

## What's New in 0.5
- **Runeforging system** — Verisium + Alloys crafting layer (see [[poe2-currency-crafting-economy]])
- **Expedition overhaul** — major rework to Expedition mechanics; Runes of Aldur is the new league mechanic
- **Breach rework** — Breach Strongholds larger, faster Wombgift consumption, 3 new named modifiers
- **Ritual rework** — more Rare/Magic spawns, Arie updated abilities
- **Delirium rework** — Simulacrums require 100% Deliriousness to spawn; new Shard system between waves
- **First Challenge League** with actual Challenges track
- **New Ascendancies** added (Druid's Oracle, others)
- **Jamanra nerf** (0.5.2): 17% less HP, petrification only once per fight

## Current Patch Timeline
- 0.5.0 launched with Return of the Ancients (May/June 2026)
- 0.5.3 — June 18, 2026 (farming improvements, Abyss/Expedition/Delirium buffs)
- 0.5.4 — current stable
