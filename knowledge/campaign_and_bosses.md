# Path of Exile 2 Campaign & Bosses

## Campaign Structure
- **6 Acts** total; 600+ monster types, 100+ bosses
- Each Act has multiple zones and a main Act boss
- Act 1 functions as a tutorial for boss mechanics
- Bosses have **telegraphed attacks** — visual cues before dangerous moves
- Final bosses per act typically have **phase transitions at 25% HP**
- Multi-phase bosses introduce new attacks/environmental effects

## Act Difficulty Progression

### Act 1 (Tutorial)
- Focus: Learning boss mechanics, telegraphed attacks
- **Cold damage is dominant** — 3 bosses deal Cold damage
- Key boss: **Count Geonor** — wolf form punishes players without Corrupting Blood management
- Tip: Cap cold resistance before Act 1 boss

### Act 2 (Notable Difficulty Spike)
- Fire + Lightning damage dominant
- Key bosses:
  - **Rathbreaker** — physical damage focus
  - **Rudja** — fire damage
  - **Jamanra the Abomination** — fire + lightning; large AoE (nerfed in 0.5.2: 17% less HP, petrification once per fight now)
- Tip: Fire flask + Lightning flask are essential here

### Act 3+
- Increased complexity: AoE attacks, minion swarms, more elemental variety
- Chaos damage starts appearing in later acts
- Keep Chaos resistance in mind for late acts

## Universal Boss Tips
1. **Be 2-3 levels above boss** — meaningfully reduces per-hit damage, more forgiving
2. **Flask setup:** Always carry Life flask + elemental resist flask matching the boss's damage type
3. **Spirit Rewards:** Every boss that drops a Spirit reward gives a **permanent stat bonus** — NEVER skip picking these up. They do not respawn.
4. **Watch for telegraphs:** All bosses signal dangerous moves visually; dodge roll away
5. **Resistances:** Check what damage type the boss deals, cap that resistance before the fight
6. **Phase awareness:** When boss HP drops to 25%, expect a phase transition with new mechanics

## Notable Named Bosses
- **Count Geonor** (Act 1) — wolf form, Corrupting Blood mechanic
- **Rathbreaker** (Act 2) — physical tank test
- **Rudja** (Act 2) — fire damage
- **Jamanra the Abomination** (Act 2) — biggest Act 2 spike; fire/lightning; nerfed in 0.5.2
- **Diamora** (late acts) — petrification (now triggers once per fight, not twice, as of 0.5.2)

## Endgame Campaign Transition
After completing all 6 acts, you gain access to the Atlas of Worlds (see [[poe2-endgame-atlas]]).

**Why:** Campaign knowledge matters early on but many mechanics carry over to endgame. Boss mechanics in the campaign directly preview endgame encounter design philosophy.
