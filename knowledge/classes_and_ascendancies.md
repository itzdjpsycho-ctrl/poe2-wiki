# Path of Exile 2 Classes & Ascendancies

As of Patch 0.5 (Return of the Ancients), 8 of 12 planned classes are available. Unrevealed classes include Marauder, Duelist, Shadow, and Templar (placeholders suggest axes, swords, daggers/thaumaturgy, and flails).

**Key principle:** Any class can use any skill gem. Classes define starting position on the passive tree and ascendancy options, not hard skill locks.

## Available Classes

### Warrior (Strength)
- **Ascendancies:** Warbringer, Titan, Smith of Kitava
- Melee specialist, heavy weapons, maces, slam skills, warcries
- Active blocking mechanic that builds stagger damage
- Great for beginners who like tanky brawlers

### Huntress (Dexterity)
- **Ascendancies:** Amazon, Ritualist, Spirit Walker
- Spear specialist, close-to-medium range
- Dynamic combo mechanics, elemental effects, bleeding applications
- Strong battlefield control

### Sorceress (Intelligence)
- **Ascendancies:** Stormweaver, Chronomancer, Disciple of Varashta
- Masters of elemental magic: fire, cold, lightning (~25 elemental skills)
- Stormweaver channels lightning; Chronomancer manipulates time
- Can dual-spec between staffs and trigger combos like ignition firestorms

### Mercenary (Strength/Dexterity)
- **Ascendancies:** Witchhunter, Gemling Legionnaire, Tactician
- Built around crossbow mechanics: Burst (shotgun), Rapid (assault rifle), Power (sniper)
- Also uses grenades; FPS-inspired playstyle
- Only class built entirely around crossbow/grenade

### Monk (Dexterity/Intelligence)
- **Ascendancies:** Invoker, Acolyte of Chayula, Martial Artist
- Highly mobile quarterstaff user, blends elemental attacks with martial arts
- Power charges, interrupt mechanics, bell-striking synergies
- Invoker is top-tier; strong endgame builds available

### Druid (Strength/Intelligence)
- **Ascendancies:** Shaman, Oracle
- Shapeshifter: transforms into Bear, Wolf, and Wyvern forms
- Can combine melee shapeshifting with spellcasting simultaneously
- e.g., Bear Form melee + lava spells from human form

### Ranger (Dexterity)
- **Ascendancies:** Deadeye, Pathfinder
- Bow specialist; lightning bounce, cold CC, poison skills, toxic clouds
- **Deadeye dominates the meta** (34%+ of top players in 0.5, 80% using Lightning Arrow)
- Pathfinder boosts flasks and poison

### Witch (Intelligence)
- **Ascendancies:** Infernalist, Blood Mage, Lich/Abyssal Lich
- Minion commander; skeletal warriors, spirit-based resurrection
- Also direct chaos damage, bone skills, curses
- Blood Mage uses life-cost scaling (strong pre-0.5, nerfed slightly in 0.5)

## Dual Specialization
Every character can "dual-specialise" their passive skill tree, switching between two completely different fighting styles with a button press. This is a core mechanic for hybrid builds.

## Weapon Specialization
Passive nodes can be specialized per weapon — unlocked during leveling/campaign. This allows your passives to shift when you switch weapons.

**Why:** This shapes which class you pick as a beginner. For new players, Warrior (tanky) and Ranger/Deadeye (map clear speed) are strongest and most forgiving in 0.5 meta.
