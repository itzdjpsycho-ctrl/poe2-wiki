# Path of Exile 2 Defensive Mechanics

## Core Defensive Layers

### Life (Foundation)
- When Life hits 0, you die
- The baseline; everything else is built on top of it
- Life flasks restore it; most recovery methods work on Life

### Armor (Physical Mitigation)
- Reduces **Physical Damage** only — not elemental
- Effective vs. many small hits; scales down vs. large single hits
- Nice to have some, but not a solo defense layer due to elemental damage being common
- Active blocking (Warrior) builds Stagger damage on enemies using shields

### Evasion (Hit Avoidance)
- As of **Patch 0.3+**, Evasion works vs. ALL hit damage (not just physical)
- Uses **weighted entropy system**: guarantees you get hit at least once every 20 attempts
- **Deflection mechanic** (added 0.3): further improves Evasion viability
- Cannot evade some special boss attacks
- Pairs very well with Energy Shield (ES covers the hits that land)

### Energy Shield (Second Health Pool)
- Acts as an extra Life pool that **sits on top of Life**
- Cannot be restored by flasks — relies on natural **ES Recharge** (kicks in after a delay without taking damage)
- Must survive the "recharge delay window" somehow (via recovery skills, Acrobatics, etc.)
- **Best synergy:** Evasion + ES — evasion keeps you from taking hits, ES absorbs the ones that land

### Runic Ward (New in 0.5)
- Introduced with the Runes of Aldur league in Patch 0.5
- **Acts as a last-resort life-saver**: if damage would reduce Life to 0 while Runic Ward is active, you drop to 1 Life instead, and Runic Ward absorbs the overflow
- Not a regenerating health bar — it's a "death prevention" mechanic
- Sourced from Runeforging (Verisium metal) applied to armor

## Resistances
- **Must cap elemental resistances at 75%** (Fire, Cold, Lightning)
- Chaos Resistance also important, especially endgame
- Each Act boss tends to emphasize a specific damage type:
  - Act 1: Cold damage bosses (cap cold res first)
  - Act 2: Fire + Lightning (Rudja, Jamanra, Rathbreaker)
  - Late game: Chaos resistance matters more

## Best Defensive Combos (Meta)
1. **Evasion + ES** — Most popular endgame hybrid; excellent synergy
2. **Armor + Life** — Strong beginner option (Warrior); good vs. physical-heavy zones
3. **Pure Life + Armor** — Simple and durable for campaign
4. **ES + Ward** — Niche, requires Runeforging investment

## Flask Usage
- Life flask is mandatory; carry always
- Elemental resist flask matched to boss = very impactful (fire flask vs. Jamanra, etc.)
- Pathfinder ascendancy buffs flask uptime significantly

**Why:** Picking a defensive layer is one of the first major build decisions. Evasion+ES is the meta for a reason — it handles both the frequency of hits and their size.
