# Path of Exile 2 Endgame: Atlas & Activities

## Atlas of Worlds
The primary endgame framework. After the 6-act campaign, you access the Atlas.
- Use **Waystones** to enter maps (replacing POE1's Map items)
- Complete maps to progress and unlock more of the Atlas
- **Tablets** drop from encounters and can enhance future map rewards/difficulty
- **Precursor Fortresses** grant Atlas Passive Tree points
- Atlas Passive Tree lets you specialize in specific league mechanics for better loot

## Pinnacle Bosses (7 total in base endgame)
Each major endgame activity has a pinnacle boss encounter:
- **Xesht, We That Are One** — Breach boss
- **Olroth, Undead Commander** — Expedition boss (Knights of the Sun)
- **Omniphobia / Kosis** — Simulacrum (Delirium) bosses
- **The King of the Mist** — Wildwood / Azmerian Wisp content
- **The Bodach** — Ritual pinnacle
- **Atziri, The Red Queen** — Incursion (Temple of Lira Vaal)
- **Vessel of Kulemak** — Abyss pinnacle

### Ultimate Boss
- **Arbiter of Ash** and **Arbiter of Divinity** — found in the Precursor Fortress
- Arbiter of Divinity = the hardest endgame boss in POE2

## League Mechanics (All in Core Game)

### Breach
- Portals spawn waves of demons; kill them to extend the timer
- Kill demons fast to keep the breach open longer
- Collect **Breach Splinters** → combine into **Breachstones** → fight Xesht
- Rewards: Catalysts (jewelry quality), Breach Rings
- Best mechanic for dense pack farming / raw currency

### Expedition
- Place explosives to detonate and reveal monsters, chests, and Remnants
- **Remnants** amplify rewards for all subsequent items in the sequence
- Collect **Logbooks** for Kalguuran expeditions (treasure island maps)
- Rewards: Runes, Verisium, Kalguuran Skills
- Key for crafting materials (runes, alloys)

### Ritual
- Sacrifice slain enemies inside circles to earn **Tribute** currency
- Spend Tribute at Arie to buy Unique items and **Omens** (crafting triggers)
- Omens include powerful effects like double-modifier Exalted Orbs
- Pinnacle: The Bodach

### Delirium / Simulacrum
- Navigate silvery fog; deeper = more dangerous and rewarding
- **Simulacrum Maps**: spawn when you reach 100% Deliriousness in fog
- Simulacrum spawns **Shards** between waves; pick 1 of 3 mid-run options
- Rewards: Distilled Emotions (for amulet/jewel enchanting)
- Key for high-tier crafting materials

### Abyss
- Close fissures by defeating emerging monsters
- Progress through three Lightless bosses → Vessel of Kulemak
- Rewards: Gnawed Bones (crafting currency)

### Incursion
- Build the Temple of Lira Vaal over 6 rooms per run
- Upgrade rooms; top tier rooms: Corruption Chamber, Alchemy Lab, Sacrificial Chamber
- Pinnacle: Atziri, The Red Queen

### Runes of Aldur (0.5 League Mechanic)
- The current Challenge League mechanic (as of June 2026)
- Carve runic recipes onto Remnants to trigger monster waves
- Slay them to collect Verisium and crafting resources
- Powers the new **Runeforging** system

## Ascendancy Trials

### Trial of Sekhemas
- Roguelike dungeon using **Honor** (separate from Life) as the health pool
- Getting hit drains Honor; lose too much = fail the trial
- Navigate challenge rooms, collect Sacred Water (heals Honor)
- Rewards: Jewels, Time-Lost Jewels (passive tree modifiers)

### Trial of Chaos
- Gauntlet: 10 consecutive challenge rooms
- Each room: Trialmaster offers a reward + a curse (stacking penalties persist)
- All 10 rooms cleared = access to Trialmaster's Tower
- Rewards: Soul Cores (gear sockets), Chaos Orbs, Vaal Orbs

## Random Map Encounters
- **Strongboxes**: Trapped loot containers with trap mechanics
- **Rogue Exiles**: Player-ability enemies; drop their visible unique items
- **Essences**: Guarantee specific affixes when used in crafting
- **Shrines**: Temporary buffs after defeating guardians
- **Corrupted Areas**: Beast-tainted zones with portal demons
- **Azmerian Wisps**: Fleeing entities that empower rare/unique monsters → Talismans

**Why:** Understanding which activities you're speccing into on your Atlas tree is the core endgame decision — it determines your currency income and what boss progression you pursue.
