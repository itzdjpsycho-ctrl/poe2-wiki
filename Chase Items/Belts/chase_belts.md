# Chase Belts — Path of Exile 2
*Stats verified against poe2db.tw*

## Mageblood
**Base:** Utility Belt | **Level Req:** 55 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Has (1–3) Charm Slots
- 20% of Flask Recovery applied Instantly
- All Mage's Legacies have (25–50)% increased effect per duplicate Mage's Legacy you have
- Legacy of Granite
- Legacy of Ruby
- Legacy of Bismuth
- Legacy of Basalt

### Why It's Powerful
- The four fixed Mage's Legacy mods each provide a powerful persistent effect based on their gem type
- Duplicating any Legacy multiplies its effect by 25–50% per copy — meaning two Legacies of the same type is dramatically stronger than one
- 20% instant flask recovery gives a strong panic-button effect on any flask used

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Ritual Tribute** | Best method | Farm Tier 16 maps with Freedom of Faith tablets; Defer if you can't afford it |
| **Orb of Chance** | Very Low | Apply to a Normal (white) Utility Belt |
| **World / Boss Drop** | Extremely Low | Any endgame content |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | Check poe.ninja for current price |

## Headhunter
**Base:** Heavy Belt | **Level Req:** 50 | **Can Be Chanced**

### Stats
- (20–30)% increased Stun Threshold
- +(40–60) to maximum Life
- +(20–40) to Strength
- +(20–40) to Dexterity
- When you kill a Rare monster, you gain its Modifiers for 60 seconds
- Has (1–3) Charm Slots

### Why It's Powerful
- Absorbing rare monster mods on kill is exponentially powerful in high-density content — after a few kills you have multiple stacked offensive and defensive bonuses
- No other belt in the game provides this level of scaling in map clearing scenarios

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Orb of Chance** | Low | Apply to a Normal (white) Heavy Belt |
| **World Drop** | Very Low | Any endgame map content |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | Significantly cheaper than Mageblood |

## Ingenuity
**Base:** Utility Belt | **Level Req:** 55 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Has (1–3) Charm Slots
- 20% of Flask Recovery applied Instantly
- (−20–20)% reduced Charm Charges gained
- (−10–10)% reduced Charm Charges used
- (20–30)% increased bonuses gained from left Equipped Ring
- (20–30)% increased bonuses gained from right Equipped Ring

### Why It's Powerful
- The ring bonus amplification is a multiplicative buff to whatever your rings provide — resistances, damage, life, crit — all increased by 20–30%
- Effectively gives you more value from two item slots without replacing them
- On a build with two powerful rings, Ingenuity outperforms most other belts

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **King in the Mists** (Ritual — Crux of Nothingness) | Only source | Cannot be chanced or world-dropped |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | High price; consistently in demand |
