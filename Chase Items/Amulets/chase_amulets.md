## Choir of the Storm
**Base:** Jade Amulet | **Level Req:** 55 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Grants Skill: Level 13 Lightning Bolt
- +(10–15) to Dexterity
- +(50–100)% to Lightning Resistance
- Critical Hits Ignore Enemy Monster Lightning Resistance
- Trigger Lightning Bolt Skill on Critical Hit

### Why It's Powerful
- Triggering Lightning Bolt on every critical hit adds a free high-damage hit with no resource cost and no button press
- Critical Hits ignoring lightning resistance is a massive DPS increase — enemies with high lightning res take full damage from your crits
- The +50–100% Lightning Resistance is one of the highest single-item resistance bonuses in the game
- Any crit-stacking lightning build uses this as a core item

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Xesht, We That Are One** (Breach — Twisted Domain) | Only source | Uncommon drop rate from Xesht |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Strong demand from lightning crit builds |

## The Pandemonius
**Base:** Lapis Amulet | **Level Req:** 52 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- +(10–15) to Intelligence
- +(50–100)% to Cold Resistance
- Damage Penetrates 75% Cold Resistance
- Blind Chilled Enemies on Hit

### Why It's Powerful
- 75% Cold Penetration is an enormous damage multiplier — enemies that would otherwise have capped cold resistance take near-full cold damage
- Blind on Chill means any chilled enemy is also blinded, giving 50% reduced enemy accuracy — strong defensive effect from a damage-focused item
- The +50–100% Cold Resistance frees resistance budget from other slots
- One of the best single items for cold builds in terms of damage and defence combined

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Xesht, We That Are One** (Breach — Twisted Domain) | Only source | Uncommon drop from Xesht |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Consistent demand from cold builds |

## Strugglescream
**Base:** Stellar Amulet | **Level Req:** 52 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- +(5–7) to all Attributes
- Can have 3 additional Instilled Modifiers

### Why It's Powerful
- Instilled Modifiers are powerful enchanted mods (from the Instilling Orb system) that are otherwise limited to 1 per item — Strugglescream allows 4 total
- This effectively turns the amulet slot into a highly customisable slot stacking up to 4 enchanted effects simultaneously
- The all-Attributes bonus is a minor but universal benefit for any build

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Simulacrum** (Delirium — Wave 15) | Only source | Very Rare drop from Simulacrum |
| **Player Trade** | High Price | Strong customisation potential keeps demand elevated |
