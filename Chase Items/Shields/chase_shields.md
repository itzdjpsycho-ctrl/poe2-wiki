## Svalinn
**Base:** Crucible Tower Shield | **Level Req:** 60 (64 Str) | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Armour: 435–580
- Ward: 50–100
- Block Chance: 26%
- +(50–100) to maximum Runic Ward
- (200–300)% increased Armour
- Chance to Block Damage is Lucky
- You take (0–20)% of Damage from Blocked Hits
- Grants Skill: Raise Shield
- Grants Skill: Level 14 Cast on Block

### Why It's Powerful
- **Lucky Block** is the single most powerful defensive modifier available on a shield — rolling two block checks and taking the better result effectively doubles your block chance in practice
- **Cast on Block** at Level 14 allows a socketed spell to fire automatically whenever you block — no button press required; combines offense and defense into one action
- **Runic Ward** integration makes this the premier shield for Runeforging builds in Patch 0.5 — Ward absorbs damage before Life and ES
- Taking 0–20% of blocked hit damage (ideally rolling 0) means some blocks deal zero damage to you entirely
