## Temporalis
**Base:** Silk Robe | **Level Req:** 64 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Energy Shield: 164–214
- +(100–150) to maximum Energy Shield
- +(10–20)% to all Elemental Resistances
- (5–30)% of Damage taken Recouped as Life
- (5–30)% of Damage taken Recouped as Mana
- Skills have (−2–−1) seconds to Cooldown

### Why It's Powerful
- The cooldown reduction on skills is the defining stat — reducing cooldowns by 1–2 seconds is enormous for any skill with a base cooldown, effectively doubling or tripling its cast rate
- Damage Recouped as both Life and Mana provides exceptional sustain — a percentage of all damage taken is returned as recovery over time
- The elemental resistance and Energy Shield make it a well-rounded defensive armour on top of the offensive cooldown benefit

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Zarokh, the Temporal** (Trial of Sekhemas — 4th Floor) with **Last Flame** relic | Only source | Must bring the Last Flame relic into the trial; no other method exists |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | One of the most expensive items in the game |

## Skin of the Loyal
**Base:** Garment | **Level Req:** 1 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- +(5–40)% to all Elemental Resistances
- Elemental Ailment Threshold is increased by Uncapped Chaos Resistance
- Armour is increased by Uncapped Fire Resistance
- Energy Shield is increased by Uncapped Cold Resistance
- Evasion Rating is increased by Uncapped Lightning Resistance

### Why It's Powerful
- All four defences scale with how much your resistances exceed the cap — intentionally over-capping resistances becomes a massive defensive multiplier
- A build with 200% overcapped Fire Resistance would double its Armour from this item alone
- Enables a unique "resistance stacking" build archetype where you invest heavily in all resistance types simultaneously

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Xesht, We That Are One** (Breach — Twisted Domain) | Only source | Rare drop from Xesht's loot table |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | Mid-range price relative to other Xesht exclusives |

## Geofri's Sanctuary
**Base:** Revered Vestments | **Level Req:** 65 | **Can Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Armour: 682–819
- Energy Shield: 76
- +1% to all Maximum Elemental Resistances
- (150–200)% increased Armour
- +(10–20)% to all Elemental Resistances
- Maximum Energy Shield is equal to (200–300)% of your Strength
- Maximum Energy Shield cannot be Converted
- Regenerate 2 Life per second for every 10 Intelligence
- Grants the keystone passive **Zealot's Oath**

### Why It's Powerful
- Converts your Strength directly into Energy Shield — a Warrior with 500 Strength gets 1000–1500 maximum Energy Shield from this alone
- Zealot's Oath causes Life Regeneration to apply to Energy Shield instead, enabling ES builds to use life regen passives efficiently
- Intelligence-based Life regen provides a strong passive healing source tied to your caster stat

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **World Drop** | Very Low | Any endgame content |
| **Orb of Chance** | Low | Apply to a Normal Revered Vestments base |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Generally affordable; strong but niche demand |

## Pragmatism
**Base:** Explorer Armour | **Level Req:** 52 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Armour: 447–596
- Evasion Rating: 396–528
- (200–300)% increased Armour and Evasion
- +(10–20)% to all Elemental Resistances
- −17% to Chaos Resistance
- Charms use no Charges

### Why It's Powerful
- Charms using no charges means all your charm slots are permanently active with zero upkeep — powerful resistances, immunity effects, and passive bonuses from charms become free
- The combined Armour and Evasion scaling makes it one of the strongest hybrid-defence armours available
- The −17% Chaos Resistance is a meaningful drawback that must be compensated for elsewhere

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **King in the Mists** (Ritual — Crux of Nothingness) | Only source | Cannot be chanced or world-dropped |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | High price; the free charm charges are uniquely powerful |

## The Auspex
**Base:** Exquisite Vest | **Level Req:** 65 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Evasion Rating: 1398–1533
- (210–240)% increased Evasion Rating
- +(70–120) to maximum Life
- 100% increased Attribute Requirements
- Grants Skill: Level 15 Mist Raven
- Chance to Deflect is Lucky while on Low Life
- Enemies in your Presence gain 1 Gruelling Madness each second

### Why It's Powerful
- Lucky Deflect on Low Life means your evasion effectively doubles when you are most in danger — the game rolls two evasion checks and takes the better result
- Gruelling Madness stacking on nearby enemies debilitates them the longer they stay near you — strong in boss fights where enemies linger
- Mist Raven is a free skill grant providing a mobility/evasion-enhancing tool
- Note: 100% increased Attribute Requirements means all gear requirements are doubled — plan your attributes carefully

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Tangmazu, The Raven Trickster** (Delirium — Withered Willow) | Only source | Uncommon drop from Tangmazu |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Strong but niche; evasion builds in demand |
