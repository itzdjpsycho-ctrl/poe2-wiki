## Rite of Passage
**Base:** Golden Charm | **Level Req:** 50 | **Can Be Chanced**

### Stats
- 15% increased Rarity of Items found
- Lasts 1 Second per use
- Consumes 80 of 80 Charges on use
- Used when you kill a Rare or Unique enemy
- Possessed by Spirit of the Azmeri for (10–20) seconds on use

### Why It's Powerful
- 15% increased Item Rarity is the highest IIR bonus available in the charm slot — farming builds stack every source of rarity and this is the best single charm contribution
- Triggered automatically on killing Rares or Uniques — in endgame maps this fires constantly with no input required
- The Azmeri Spirit possession buff lasts up to 20 seconds, providing passive bonuses during that window
- Pairs well with Ventor's Gamble (ring) for maximum IIR farming setups

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Azmerian Wisp-possessed enemies** | Low | Only drops from monsters possessed by Azmerian Wisps; first appear in The Grelwood, Act 1 |
| **Endgame high-density maps** | Best farming method | Higher map tier = denser wisp population |
| **Player Trade** | Easy | Reasonably priced; widely available |
