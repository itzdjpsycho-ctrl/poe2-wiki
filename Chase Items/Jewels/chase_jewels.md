## The Adorned
**Base:** Diamond (Vaal Jewel) | **Level Req:** 20 | **Corrupted** | **Limited to 1** | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- (0–150)% increased Effect of Jewel Socket Passive Skills containing Corrupted Magic Jewels
- (0–75)% increased Effect of Jewel Socket Passive Skills containing Corrupted Rare Jewels
- Various negative implicit mods ranging (−30–30)% that can include: reduced Physical Damage, reduced Fire/Cold/Lightning/Chaos Damage, reduced Armour, reduced Evasion Rating, reduced maximum Energy Shield, Minions deal reduced Damage

### Why It's Powerful
- At the maximum roll, Corrupted Magic jewels in your passive tree have their effects amplified by 150% — doubling and then some
- Corrupted Rare jewels get up to 75% increased effect — still a massive multiplier
- The negative mods are the cost — you roll The Adorned itself using currency and hope for a weak negative mod while maximising the jewel effect percentage
- The entire "jewel-stacking" build archetype exists because of this item

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **The Trialmaster** (Trial of Chaos) | Only source | Very Rare — one of the hardest drops in the game |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | Extremely expensive; one of the highest-priced items per league |

## Heroic Tragedy
**Base:** Timeless Jewel | **Level Req:** 20 | **Corrupted** | **Limited to 1 Historic** | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Remembrancing (100–8000) songworthy deeds by the line of Vorana
- Passives in Radius are Conquered by the Kalguur
- Radius: Very Large (1500)
- Modifies 40 passives within radius, transforming them into Kalguuran-themed notables
- Grants one keystone depending on faction influence:
  - **Black Scythe Training** (Vorana): Gain no inherent bonus from Strength; 1% increased Energy Shield per 2 Strength
  - **Circular Teachings** (Medved): Gain no inherent bonus from Dexterity; 1% increased Armour per 2 Dexterity
  - **Knightly Tenets** (Olroth): Gain no inherent bonus from Intelligence; 1% increased Evasion Rating per 2 Intelligence

### Why It's Powerful
- Transforms 40 passive nodes in a large radius into Kalguuran notables — effectively rewriting a significant portion of your passive tree
- The granted keystone changes your entire attribute conversion, enabling unusual defensive scaling (e.g. pure Intelligence characters gaining Evasion from Strength)
- The number of "deeds" determines which passive nodes are affected and in what way — higher values affect more impactful nodes

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Olroth, Origin of the Fall** (Expedition — Ruins of Kingsmarch) | Only source | Rare drop from Olroth |
| **Player Trade** | High Price | Build-defining; very sought after |

## Prism of Belief
**Base:** Diamond | **Level Req:** 20 | **Corrupted** | **Limited to 1** | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- +(1–3) to Level of all Specific Skill Skills

### Why It's Powerful
- Adding gem levels to all Specific Skill gems is a universal DPS increase — every active skill you use benefits
- At +3 levels, skills effectively gain the equivalent of 3 quality tiers of power all at once
- No other single jewel provides this breadth of gem-level scaling

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Arbiter of Ash** (Citadel Pinnacle Boss) | Only source | Very Rare from Arbiter of Ash's loot table |
| **Player Trade** | High Price | Universal power makes it sought by all skill-based builds |

## Controlled Metamorphosis
**Base:** Diamond | **Level Req:** 20 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Radius: Very Small (800)
- Only affects Passives in Medium-Large Ring
- Passives in Radius can be Allocated without being connected to your tree
- −(20–5)% to all Elemental Resistances

### Why It's Powerful
- Allowing unconnected passive allocation in a radius means you can pick up isolated clusters of powerful passives without needing to path through the tree to reach them
- Effectively adds 5–15+ free passive points worth of value depending on what isolated notables or keystones are nearby in your planned tree
- The resistance penalty is the trade-off — plan your resistance budget accordingly

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Xesht, We That Are One** (Breach — Twisted Domain) | Only source | Rare drop from Xesht |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Strong but niche demand |

## Megalomaniac
**Base:** Diamond | **Level Req:** 20 | **Corrupted** | **Limited to 1** | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Allocates a random Notable Passive Skill (appears 2–3 times; cannot duplicate the same passive)
- 2-mod version: 2 random notables
- 3-mod version: 3 random notables (Extremely Rare)

### Why It's Powerful
- Each allocated notable is as powerful as if you had spent a passive point on it in the tree
- A 3-mod Megalomaniac with three build-defining notables can free up 15+ passive points or provide access to notables that are unreachable on your tree path
- The value is entirely dependent on the combination rolled — some combos are worth Divines, others are nearly worthless

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Simulacrum** (Delirium — Wave 15) | Only source | 2-mod: Very Rare; 3-mod: Extremely Rare |
| **Player Trade** | Variable | Price entirely based on which notables rolled |

## Grand Spectrum
**Base:** Ruby / Emerald / Sapphire Jewel | **Level Req:** 20 | **Limited to 3** | **Cannot Be Chanced**

Three variants that stack bonuses based on how many Grand Spectrum jewels you have socketed total.

### Stats — Grand Spectrum (Ruby)
- 2% increased Maximum Life per Grand Spectrum socketed

### Stats — Grand Spectrum (Emerald)
- 2% increased Spirit per Grand Spectrum socketed

### Stats — Grand Spectrum (Sapphire)
- +6% to all Elemental Resistances per Grand Spectrum socketed

### Why It's Powerful
- With all 3 variants socketed: each jewel counts the other two, giving +4% Life, +4% Spirit, and +12% to all resistances from three jewel sockets
- All three bonuses simultaneously — life, spirit, and resistances — are universally valuable stats
- Limited to 3 total means you can fill all three slots with the same type for a single-stat focus, or mix for balance

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Trial of the Sekhemas** (Zarokh — 4th Floor caches) | Only source | Found in reward caches during the Trial |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | All three variants commonly available; mid-tier price |

## Split Personality
**Base:** Ruby Jewel | **Level Req:** 20 | **Corrupted** | **Limited to 1** | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Can Allocate Passive Skills from other class starting points (Sorceress, Warrior, Ranger, Mercenary, Templar, Shadow)

### Why It's Powerful
- Normally you can only allocate passives connected to your own class starting area — Split Personality removes this restriction for one additional starting point
- Allows access to powerful keystones or notable clusters that are otherwise completely out of reach on your character's side of the tree
- Enables true hybrid builds or access to defensive/offensive nodes from the opposite side of the passive tree

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Tangmazu, The Raven Trickster** (Delirium — Withered Willow) | Only source | Rare drop |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Sought after for hybrid builds |
