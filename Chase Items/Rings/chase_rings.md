## Ventor's Gamble
**Base:** Gold Ring | **Level Req:** 64 | **Can Be Chanced**

### Stats
- (6–15)% increased Rarity of Items found
- +(0–80) to maximum Life
- +(0–20) to Spirit
- (−25–25)% reduced Rarity of Items found *(can be negative, offsetting the IIR above)*
- (−40–40)% to Fire Resistance
- (−40–40)% to Cold Resistance
- (−40–40)% to Lightning Resistance

### What Makes a "Good" Ventor's
- All three resistances rolling positive + high net IIR = top-tier farming ring
- The IIR and the resistance mods all have independent ranges — a high IIR roll with positive resistances is extremely rare
- Even negative-resistance versions can be used if you can compensate elsewhere
- Check the net IIR (first mod minus second mod) when evaluating a roll

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Orb of Chance** | Low | Apply to a Normal (white) Gold Ring |
| **World Drop** | Very Low | Any endgame content |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Price varies wildly by roll quality |
