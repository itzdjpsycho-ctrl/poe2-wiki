## The Burden of Shadows
**Base:** Chiming Staff | **Level Req:** 78 (137 Int) | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- (20–30)% increased Cast Speed
- Skill Mana Costs Converted to Life Costs
- Skills gain 1% of Damage as Chaos Damage per 3 Life Cost
- Grants Skill: Level 18 Sigil of Power

### Why It's Powerful
- Converting Mana costs to Life costs enables an entirely different resource economy — combined with strong Life regeneration and leech, you can cast indefinitely without Mana
- 1% Chaos damage per 3 Life cost scales directly with how expensive your skills are — high-cost skills output enormous Chaos damage
- Sigil of Power grant is a free high-level spell that provides a lasting damage buff zone
- Enables caster builds that invest entirely in Life rather than Mana

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **King in the Mists** (Ritual — Crux of Nothingness) | Only source | Rare drop from King in the Mists |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | Strong but niche demand |

## Olrovasara
**Base:** Torment Club | **Level Req:** 65 (114 Str) | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Physical Damage: 44–73
- Lightning Damage: 1 to (60–80)
- Critical Hit Chance: 5%
- Attacks per Second: (1.52–1.89)
- Weapon Range: 1.3
- +(300–400) to Accuracy Rating
- (5–30)% increased Attack Speed
- On hitting an enemy, gains maximum added lightning damage equal to the enemy's power for 20 seconds, up to a total of 500

### Why It's Powerful
- The "enemy's power" mechanic means hitting stronger enemies gives more lightning damage — in endgame maps you rapidly stack up to 500 added lightning damage, which persists for 20 seconds
- High accuracy rating removes the need for accuracy investment elsewhere
- The attack speed range is wide — a high roll transforms this into an exceptionally fast mace

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Olroth, Origin of the Fall** (Expedition — Ruins of Kingsmarch) | Only source | Common drop from Olroth |
| **Player Trade** | Easy | Frequently available; affordable |

## Brutus' Lead Sprinkler
**Base:** Morning Star | **Level Req:** 45 (80 Str) | **Can Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Physical Damage: (59–73) to (88–108)
- Critical Hit Chance: 6.5%
- Attacks per Second: (1.52–1.60)
- Weapon Range: 1.3
- Grants Skill: Level 11 Molten Shower
- Hits with this Weapon have 5% chance to Trigger Molten Shower per 25 Strength
- (80–120)% increased Physical Damage
- (5–10)% increased Attack Speed
- +(15–25) to Strength
- 5–10 Added Attack Fire Damage per 25 Strength

### Why It's Powerful
- Scales fire damage directly from Strength — with 500 Strength you add 100–200 fire damage per hit
- The Molten Shower trigger fires a lava rain AoE for free on hits — at high Strength this triggers frequently without any skill investment
- Grants Molten Shower as a usable active skill even if you wouldn't otherwise have access
- Excellent campaign and early endgame weapon for Strength-stacking Warriors

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Orb of Chance** | Low | Apply to a Normal Morning Star |
| **World Drop** | Low | Any content |
| **Player Trade** | Easy | Very affordable |

## Redemption
**Base:** Trarthan Cannon | **Level Req:** 65 (114 Str, 63 Dex) | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Physical Damage: (232–290) to (536–670)
- Critical Hit Chance: 5%
- Attacks per Second: 1.4
- Cannot load or fire Ammunition
- (300–400)% increased Physical Damage
- Hits with this Weapon have no Critical Damage Bonus
- (20–40)% reduced Cooldown Recovery Rate
- Gain 1 Explosive Rhythm every (2–3) times you use a Grenade Skill; Remove all Explosive Rhythm on reaching 10 to gain Explosive Fervour for 10 seconds

### Why It's Powerful
- Explosive Fervour is a powerful damage buff unlocked after 10 Grenade Skill uses — this creates a rhythm of building up and releasing burst windows
- Cannot load Ammunition means it fires via the Explosive Fervour mechanic only — a different playstyle from standard crossbows
- The (300–400)% physical damage increase is among the highest on any weapon in the game
- Designed specifically for Grenade-focused builds; less useful outside of that archetype

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **World Drop / Boss Drop** | Low | Drops as an endgame unique; no specific boss confirmed |
| **Player Trade** | Moderate | New in 0.5; price fluctuates |
