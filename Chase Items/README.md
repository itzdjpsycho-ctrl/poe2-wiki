## General Acquisition Tips

- **Cannot be chanced:** Hand of Wisdom and Action, Temporalis, Ingenuity, Pragmatism, Heroic Tragedy, The Adorned — 100% boss-exclusive
- **Best chanceable chase items:** Mageblood (Utility Belt), Headhunter (Heavy Belt), Ventor's Gamble (Gold Ring), Brutus' Lead Sprinkler (Morning Star)
- **Ritual farming** is the primary method for Mageblood and Ingenuity — use Freedom of Faith tablets, chain maps with Head of the King
- **poe.ninja** — check live prices before committing to farming a specific boss
- **poe2db.tw** — authoritative source for exact item stats and drop sources
