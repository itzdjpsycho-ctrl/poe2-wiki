## Assailum
**Base:** Closed Helm | **Level Req:** 45 (36 Str, 36 Dex) | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Armour: 384–512
- Evasion Rating: 345–460
- (200–300)% increased Armour and Evasion
- +(200–400) to Accuracy Rating
- (30–50)% increased Critical Hit Chance
- Skills have a (100–150)% longer Perfect Timing window

### Why It's Powerful
- "Perfect Timing" in POE2 is the mechanic for landing precision strikes at the optimal moment — a longer window makes it dramatically easier to hit Perfect Timing consistently
- At 150% longer window you can reliably trigger Perfect Timing on almost every attack without precise execution
- The Critical Hit Chance bonus further amplifies the damage ceiling
- Strong on any melee build that uses skills with a Perfect Timing mechanic

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Simulacrum** (Delirium — Wave 15) | Only source | Common drop from Simulacrum — most accessible Simulacrum unique |
| **Player Trade** | Easy | Lower price among Simulacrum drops |

## Keeper of the Arc
**Base:** Spiritbone Crown | **Level Req:** 62 (36 Str, 36 Int) | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Armour: 435–563
- Energy Shield: 125–162
- (240–340)% increased Armour and Energy Shield
- (15–25) Life Regeneration per second
- (15–25)% increased Mana Regeneration Rate
- Alternating every 5 seconds: Take 40% less Damage from Hits / Take 40% less Damage over Time

### Why It's Powerful
- The alternating 40% less damage reduction is enormous — every 5 seconds you are either highly resistant to burst damage or highly resistant to DoT damage
- In practice this means you take 40% less damage from all sources if you time your defensive windows correctly, or use abilities that align with the active phase
- Life regen and Mana regen are both strong sustain bonuses that don't require flask use
- Exceptional in endgame boss fights where hit patterns are learnable

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Olroth, Origin of the Fall** (Expedition — Ruins of Kingsmarch) | Only source | Common drop from Olroth's table |
| **Player Trade** | Easy | Affordable; frequently available from Expedition farming |
