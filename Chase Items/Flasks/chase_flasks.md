## Olroth's Resolve
**Base:** Ultimate Life Flask | **Level Req:** 60 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Recovers 920 Life over 3 seconds
- Consumes 10 of 75 charges on use
- (100–150)% increased Charges per use
- Regenerate (2.5–5)% of maximum Runic Ward per second during Effect
- Gain Guard equal to Current Runic Ward for 10 seconds when Effect ends

### Why It's Powerful
- Regenerating Runic Ward during the flask effect restores a significant defensive layer without combat being required — powerful in boss fights where you have brief safe windows
- Gaining Guard equal to your full current Runic Ward when the effect ends gives a burst of temporary protection right after the flask expires — essentially the flask provides both a recovery window and a defensive burst on its end
- The increased charges per use means you can use this flask more times per map than a standard flask before needing to recharge
- Exceptionally strong in Patch 0.5's Runeforging meta where Runic Ward is a primary defensive layer

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Olroth, Origin of the Fall** (Expedition — Ruins of Kingsmarch) | Only source | Extremely Rare — the hardest drop from Olroth by far |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | Among the most expensive flasks in any given league |

### Farming Olroth
1. Farm Expedition content in Tier 15–16 maps using Kingsmarch Expedition Tablets
2. Olroth's full loot table: Shattered Triskelion (always), Keeper of the Arc (common), Olrovasara (common), Svalinn (rare), Heroic Tragedy (rare), **Olroth's Resolve** (extremely rare)
3. Stack Item Rarity on character and in maps for the best odds
4. Consider buying from trade if you have the currency — the farming time cost is very high
