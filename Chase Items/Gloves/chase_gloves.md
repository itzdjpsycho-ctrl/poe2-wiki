## Hand of Wisdom and Action
**Base:** Spiral Wraps | **Level Req:** 33 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- Evasion Rating: 43
- Energy Shield: 15
- +(15–25) to Dexterity
- +(15–25) to Intelligence
- 1% increased Attack Speed per 20 Dexterity
- Adds 1 to 10 Lightning Damage to Attacks per 20 Intelligence

### Why It's Powerful
- Every 20 Intelligence adds 1–10 flat lightning damage to all attacks — with 300 Intelligence that's 15–150 added lightning damage per hit before any multipliers
- Every 20 Dexterity adds 1% increased Attack Speed — with 300 Dexterity that's +15% attack speed from a single stat
- Hybrid attribute stacking (Int + Dex) becomes a build archetype entirely around these gloves
- Best suited for Deadeye (natural Dex) with high Intelligence investment, or Monk (natural Int) investing in Dexterity

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Xesht, We That Are One** (Breach — Twisted Domain) | Only source | Rare exclusive drop — cannot be chanced |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | High price; demand driven by multiple S-tier builds |

## Hateforge
**Base:** Moulded Mitts | **Level Req:** 60 | **Cannot Be Chanced**

### Stats
- (300–400)% increased Armour
- Gain (3–6) Rage on Hit
- (−10–10) to Maximum Rage
- Gain a random Charge when you reach Maximum Rage (no more than once every 3–6 seconds)
- Lose all Rage on reaching Maximum Rage

### Why It's Powerful
- Provides a steady engine of Rage generation on any hit — builds that attack quickly ramp to maximum Rage very fast
- Gaining a random Charge (Power, Frenzy, or Endurance) at maximum Rage gives free offensive or defensive charge access without the usual generation investment
- The Rage reset after charging means you cycle continuously, gaining a charge every time you cap
- Extremely strong on fast-attacking builds — the more you attack, the more charges you generate

### How to Obtain
| Method | Reliability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **The Trialmaster** (Trial of Chaos) | Only source | Very Rare drop |
| **Player Trade** | Guaranteed | High price; build-enabling for Rage builds |
