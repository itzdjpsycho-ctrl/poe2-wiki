# Path of Exile 2 Currency, Crafting & Economy

## Currency Hierarchy

### Primary Trading Currencies
- **Exalted Orbs** — primary trade currency (benchmark for pricing)
- **Divine Orbs** — high-value; rerolls values of explicit item modifiers. More common in 0.5 than before (price relative to Exalted has shifted)
- **Chaos Orbs** — rerolls all mods on a rare item randomly; used in bulk trades

### Crafting Orbs
- **Orb of Transmutation** — Normal → Magic item (adds 1-2 mods)
- **Orb of Augmentation** — adds a mod to a Magic item
- **Orb of Regal** — Magic → Rare item (adds 1 mod)
- **Exalted Orb** — adds 1 mod to a Rare item
- **Chaos Orb** — rerolls all mods on Rare item (RNG)
- **Divine Orb** — rerolls the values of existing mods (min/max range)
- **Vaal Orb** — corrupts an item (random outcome, can brick or superpower; item becomes uncraftable)
- **Orb of Annulment** — removes 1 random mod from an item
- **Jeweller's Orbs** (Lesser, Greater, Perfect) — modify gem sockets
- **Gemcutter's Prism** — improves skill gem quality

### Support Currency
- **Gold** — used for NPC trades and respec-ing passive points (NOT for player trading)
- **Essences** — guarantee a specific affix when used on an item
- **Catalysts** — improve quality on jewelry (from Breach)

### New in Patch 0.5
- **Verisium Metal** — new crafting material dropped by Remnant-activated monsters; used for Runeforging
- **Alloys** (13 types) — deterministic crafting; replace an existing modifier with a guaranteed targeted one (like Perfect Essences for specific stats)
- **Gnawed Bones** — Abyss crafting currency

## Crafting Progressions

### Basic Crafting Path for Rare Items
1. Find a high-value item base
2. Transmute → Augment → Regal to get a 3-mod Rare
3. Exalt to add more mods
4. Use Annulments to remove bad mods
5. Divine to fix low values on good mods

### Runeforging (0.5 System)
- **What it is:** Engrave armor with Runic Ward properties using Verisium
- **Runic Ward:** Last-resort death prevention — if damage would kill you, you drop to 1 Life and Runic Ward absorbs the rest
- **Remnants** in maps drop Verisium and can be used to carve specific rune recipes
- **Unique Verisium Runeforging** (unlocked Act 3): Upgrades low-level Unique weapons/armor base types (below item level 55)
- **Alloys** = Overwrite existing modifiers with deterministic crafted mods

### Incursion Crafting (Temple of Lira Vaal)
- **Corruption Chamber**: Vaal-corrupts items with specific outcomes
- **Alchemy Lab**: Upgrades item quality
- **Sacrificial Chamber**: Currency sacrificing for upgrade currency

## Trading Systems

### Currency Exchange
- Bulk currency exchange via in-game market (talk to Alva in Ziggurat Refuge)
- Use it to **price-check** before selling: place buy orders to see market rates
- Three trade channels in 0.5: Currency Exchange, hideout stash trading, and P2P whispers

### Player Trading
- **Dump tab strategy**: Set a public stash tab to a fixed price (e.g. 2 Exalted Orbs) and fill it — items that look worthless often sell
- Standard pricing currency: Exalted Orbs; high-value items priced in Divines
- Use poe.ninja and trade site to check current prices

## Economy Tips
- **Income formula:** Income = Time Spent × Efficiency
- Minimize hideout time; maximize monster killing
- **Breach** is best for raw currency from dense packs
- **Expedition/Runes of Aldur** is best for crafting material farming
- **Ritual Omens** (esp. double-modifier Exalted) are extremely valuable — sell or use on high-end gear
- Divine Orbs are more common in 0.5 — great for endgame min-maxing but their trade value has dropped

**Why:** The economy determines how fast you can complete your build. Understanding which currency to hoard vs. spend vs. trade is the difference between struggling and thriving in endgame.
